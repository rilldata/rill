# Rill User Guide

Rill Cloud helps business users and data analysts explore and share insights without needing to write SQL. This guide covers how to use Rill's interactive dashboards and collaboration features in the cloud.

## Explore
Learn how to find answers in your data using Rill's core features.
- **[Dashboard Basics](/user-guide/explore/dashboard-101)** - Navigate the Explore dashboard interface, understand metrics and dimensions, and learn how to use the dimension leaderboard to spot trends.
- **[Filtering](/user-guide/explore/filters)** - Apply include/exclude filters, use time ranges to isolate specific periods, and leverage regular expressions for advanced data selection.
- **[Time Series Analysis](/user-guide/explore/time-series)** - Drill down into time intervals (hour, day, week, etc.), compare current performance against historical periods, and visualize trends with interactive charts.
- **[Dimension Detail](/user-guide/explore/dashboard-101/tdd)** - Dive deeper into specific dimensions to see time-series breakdowns for individual values.
- **[Pivot Tables](/user-guide/explore/dashboard-101/pivot)** - Create cross-tabulations to analyze relationships between multiple dimensions and metrics.

## AI Analysis
Use natural language to query your data.
- **[AI Chat](/user-guide/explore/ai-chat)** - Use Rill's built-in conversational interface to ask questions about your data in plain English and get instant charts and insights.
- **[MCP Server](/user-guide/explore/mcp)** - Connect Rill to external AI assistants like Claude Desktop to perform complex analysis and reasoning over your metrics.

## Collaborate
Share insights and keep your team informed.
- **[Bookmarks](/user-guide/explore/bookmarks)** - Capture the exact state of your dashboard (filters, time range, selected metrics) to share with colleagues or save for later.
- **[Alerts](/user-guide/explore/alerts)** - Set up automated notifications (email or Slack) triggered when specific metrics cross a defined threshold or meet custom criteria.
- **[Scheduled Reports](/user-guide/explore/exports)** - Configure recurring email delivery of dashboard snapshots and data exports to keep stakeholders up to date.

## Manage
Administer your organization and projects on Rill Cloud.
- **[User Management](/user-guide/manage/user-management)** - Invite team members to your organization, assign roles (Admin, Viewer, Editor), and control access to specific projects.
- **[Project Settings](/user-guide/manage/project-management)** - Manage project-level configurations, including public access settings, resource usage, and general metadata.
